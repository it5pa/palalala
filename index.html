se ti chiamo, ti giri? is a 110 x 175 mm publication made for vieni fortuna and is composed of text contributions by writers, poets, musicians, artists. Launched in Sep 2024 at Museo Macro, self-published.

they love the idea of me is an A4 publication composed of around 250 images and 12 short essays edited by Jonas Morgenthaler and Morganne Billuart, self-published in 2023.

Visual identity for the LungA Art Festival made in collaboration with Greta Thorkels. Here the festival's poster, film screening booklet, parts of programme announcements and bits of merch.

Digital graphic for nEYEYEght, an exhibition held at Galeria Francisco Fino.

Going Somewhere is a 110 x 180 mm book with a screenprinted cloth cover made in collaboration with Otso Peräsaari and published by EKA GD MA in 2022. Here, also pictured a special napkin produced for the book launch in 2022.

Just Before Something Happens, Just After Something Happens, Something Happens is a publication the length of the Latvian alphabet and contains writings about falling and making, recording and holes. 130 x 190 mm, self-published, 2022.

Tracks as Envelopes is a website as a multi-track audio player, an archive of 44 audio-letters recorded in situ by voices of the visitors at the Dear Friend exhibition.

Cover illustration for dose magazine, 2023.

Poster series for an application call made with hand-worked offset printing plates, with Louise Borinski for EKA GD MA, B2, 2021. Selected 100 Beste Plakate Winner 2022.

Digital exhibition poster for Jonas Morgenthaler, 2022.

Project Exit Strategy was a 6 day trip on a self-built raft, that ended up in a three-part publication compiled of raft diaries, a photo book and an audio cassette. With Aleksandrs Breže, 2022.
